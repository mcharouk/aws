# Product Overview

This repository contains training materials and demonstrations for AWS "Architecting on AWS" and "Technical Essentials" courses. The content is structured as a comprehensive teaching resource covering AWS services across 13 modules, from infrastructure fundamentals to advanced topics like serverless, containers, and disaster recovery.

## Purpose

- Instructor-led AWS training materials with demos, labs, and customer success stories
- Hands-on demonstrations of AWS services and architectural patterns
- Teaching resources organized by module with synopsis documents, whiteboards, and practical examples
- Focus on AWS Well-Architected Framework principles and best practices

## Target Audience

The materials are designed for AWS training courses delivered in French, targeting architects and developers learning AWS cloud architecture and services.
